Description:

The Main Menu allows you to navigate through the scoreboard, the options, and the start button.
After selecting a game mode, you can start. In the options menu, you can set and select different
aspects of the game to cater to your experience. The scoreboard features the names and scores
of the individuals who have played.

Instructions:

Use the A & D keys to move your cannon around. Use the SPACE key to fire laser bullets at the incoming
aliens. Fight through each wave to achieve a new high score!
